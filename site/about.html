About the Calligrapher

Hi, I'm Mirabelle, the calligrapher behind Cherished Calligraphy. I specialize in hand-lettering for weddings, brand activations, corporate events, and meaningful celebrations.

Cherished Calligraphy was created from a love of intentional design and the belief that small details leave a lasting impression. From holiday and place cards to live engraving and custom pieces, every project is approached with care, precision, and a deep respect for the moment it represents.

When I'm not creating, you can usually find me testing new tools, refining scripts, or exploring Dallas/Fort Worth with an oat milk latte in hand.
